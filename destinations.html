Tosh Kheerganga Kalga

"One's destination is never a place but always a new way of seeing things!" Tired of hustle and bustle of every day of a metro life? Bored with your monotonous Schedule? Up for a break?

Travel with us to the Tosh-Kheerganga-Kalga - ‘Himachal’s Best Kept Secret’ blessed with a wealth of nature’s treasures. A paradise for trekkers, bird lovers, and adventure seekers!

ITINERARY FOR TRIP
What follows is a general flow of events. Expect the unexpected and prepare to be flexible.

  • Day 1

    Onboarding from Kashmere Gate
    Drop at Tosh
    Check-in at your comfortable hotel
    Refresh Yourself with some hot tea and splendid views
    Take some rest
    Small treks to best points in Tosh
    Bonfire, lights, and music with some fun activities.

  • Day 2

    Leave for Barsheni
    Embrace for the amazing trek to Kheerganga.
    Rejuvenate and relax in the sulfurous hot spring right under the snow-covered mountains.
    Take some rest Experience the stars and galaxy like never before.
    Have a comfy sleep in your tent.

  • Day 3

    Trek down to Kalga
    Get comfortable in cosy homestay with the locals.
    Relax and explore the village.
    Take some rest
    Enjoy the local cuisine before we start some bonfire and set up some music to groove.

  • Day 4

    Spend sometime in Kasol and enjoy the
    famous German bakery

    Return to Delhi
